Feedback

The developers and maintainers of Mozilla Archive Format welcome your feedback, that is valuable for the continuous improvement of the software. Whether you want to suggest a new feature, report a bug, or just ask a question, we'd be glad to hear from you.

Subscribe to the mailing list

Requires a mail client and a valid e-mail address.

This is the recommended option.

When you are subscribed to the mailing list, just send a message to maf@mozdev.org.

Use the newsgroup interface

Requires a newsgroup client.

To prevent spam, access to the newsgroup is protected by username and password.

  • Username: mozdev
  • Password: maduser

More information

In addition to the mailing list, the MAF project also runs a bug tracker. If you would like to see the list of bug reports, or view the previous discussions on the mailing list, you can do so from the bugs page and the mailing list page. The known issues with the latest version are listed in the download page.
